About Sasha

Before the full-scale invasion, Sasha was engaged in educational activities: he founded the Mathfit project - an educational project to popularize Olympiad mathematics, taught in math circles and helped with the Olympiads. When Russia invaded Ukraine, Sasha was studying in America at MIT. There, together with other Ukrainians, he organized events in support of Ukraine, communicated with journalists, etc. Later, Sasha decided to help the military with support and returned to Ukraine, where he founded the foundation.
